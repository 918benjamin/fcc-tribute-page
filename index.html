Emanuel Lasker

Longest reign as World Chess Champion

A brief timeline of Emanuel Lasker's life:

  • 1868 - Born in Neumark (now Barlinek in Poland)
  • 1892 - Founded his first chess magazine, The London Chess Fortnighly
  • 1893 - Won all 13 games in a NYC chess tournament, a rare perfect score
  • 1894 - Beat reigning World Champion Wilhelm Steinitz with ten wins, five losses, and four draws
  • 1941 - Died at the age of 72 in New York
